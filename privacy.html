Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Wavelength ("we", "us", "our") operates the Wavelength mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Data: Email address and password (or Apple ID) when you create an account.
  • Profile Data: Display name and profile photo you provide.
  • Group Data: Groups you create or join, group names, and member information.
  • Event Data: Events you create, your availability selections, and calendar preferences.
  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, and app version for crash reporting and compatibility.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To enable group creation, event planning, and availability coordination with your friends.
  • To send push notifications about event updates and reminders.
  • To improve the app experience and fix bugs.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in these cases:

  • Group Members: Your display name, profile photo, and availability selections are visible to other members of your groups.
  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for authentication and data storage. Data is stored in Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure.
  • Payment Processing: If you subscribe to Wavelength Pro, payment is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not store payment card details.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase servers with encryption at rest and in transit. We use Row Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure users can only access data within their groups.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate data via your profile settings.
  • Delete your account and all associated data. See our Data Deletion page.
  • Export your data by contacting us.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data (profile, group memberships, availability data) is permanently deleted.

8. Children's Privacy

Wavelength is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
