Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Thinq ("we", "us", "our") operates the Thinq mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Data: Email address and password when you create an account.
  • Profile Data: Name, age, bio, and profile photos you provide during setup.
  • Test Results: Your IQ test score, IQ cluster, mindset cluster, and mindset axis scores. These are used exclusively for matching purposes.
  • Usage Data: Swipe actions (like/pass), matches, and messages sent within the app.
  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, and app version for crash reporting and compatibility.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To match you with compatible users based on IQ cluster and mindset profile.
  • To enable communication between matched users.
  • To improve the app experience and fix bugs.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in these cases:

  • Other Users: Your name, age, bio, photos, IQ cluster, and mindset cluster are visible to other users for matching purposes. Your exact IQ score is never shown to other users.
  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for authentication and data storage. Data is stored in Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase servers with encryption at rest and in transit. We use Row Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure users can only access their own data and data shared with them through matches.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate data via your profile settings.
  • Delete your account and all associated data. See our Data Deletion page.
  • Export your data by contacting us.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data (profile, test results, messages, swipes, matches) is permanently deleted within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Thinq is not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we learn that a user is under 18, their account will be immediately deleted.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
