Delete My Data

Last updated: April 8, 2026

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. Here's how:

Option 1: In-App Deletion

Steps:

  1. Open Thinq and go to the Profile tab.
  2. Tap the Settings icon (top right).
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted.

Option 2: Email Request

Steps:

  1. Send an email to support@thinq-app.com
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address associated with your account.
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile (name, age, bio, photos)
  • IQ test results and mindset assessment scores
  • All swipe history (likes and passes)
  • All matches
  • All messages sent and received
  • Your authentication credentials

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will need to create a new account and retake the IQ and mindset tests if you wish to use Thinq again.

Timeline

Data is marked for deletion immediately and permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. During this period, your profile will no longer be visible to other users.
