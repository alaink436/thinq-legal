Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a group?

Tap the + button on the Groups tab, choose a name and emoji, and your group is ready. Share the invite code with friends.

How do I join a group?

Tap the join icon in the Groups tab header, enter the invite code you received, and you're in.

How does the availability overlap work?

When all members enter their availability, Wavelength shows a heatmap of the best dates. Darker colors mean more people are available. The group admin can then confirm the best date.

Can I add confirmed events to my calendar?

Yes! Once an event is confirmed, tap "Add to Calendar" to save it directly to your device's native calendar.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free users can create up to 3 groups with 5 events each. Pro unlocks unlimited groups, unlimited events, custom templates, and priority support.

How do I leave a group?

Open the group, scroll down, and tap "Leave Group". If you're the admin, you'll need to delete the group instead.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account, or visit our Data Deletion page.
