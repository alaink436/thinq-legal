Frequently Asked Questions

Can I retake the IQ test?

No. Each account gets one test attempt to ensure fairness. The adaptive algorithm provides an accurate assessment in a single session.

Who can see my IQ score?

Nobody. Your exact IQ score is private. Other users only see your IQ cluster (A-E) and mindset type. This keeps matching fair without creating score anxiety.

How does matching work?

Thinq matches you with users who have similar IQ clusters and compatible mindset profiles. You'll primarily see users within your IQ range and with complementary worldviews.

Is the IQ test scientifically validated?

The test is designed for entertainment and matching purposes. It covers 5 cognitive domains (Logic, Pattern, Verbal, Math, Spatial) with adaptive difficulty. While inspired by psychometric principles, it is not a clinical IQ test.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account, or visit our Data Deletion page for instructions.

How do I report a user?

Open the chat with the user, tap the menu icon (…) in the top right, and select "Report". You can also email us at support@thinq-app.com with the user's name and details.

Is Thinq free?

The core experience (test, matching, messaging) is free. Premium features may be introduced in the future.
